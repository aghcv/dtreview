Searches identified 3,314 records: 2,898 from PubMed, Web of Science, and IEEE Xplore and 416 from Google Scholar, arXiv, and medRxiv. After 752 duplicate records and five additional records were removed before screening, 2,557 titles and abstracts were screened; 2,307 were excluded, 250 reports were sought, three could not be retrieved, and 247 full texts were assessed. The final evidence base comprised 64 coded review records representing 62 unique normalized titles, with 183 reports excluded at full-text review. Publication accelerated sharply near the end of the search window: 28 records were published in 2025, 21 in 2024, nine in 2023, and six in 2022, so 49 of 64 records (76.6\%) appeared in 2024--2025.
